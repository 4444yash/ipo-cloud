💡 Smart IPO Bidding Cheat Sheet (Beginners)

🎯 Tick "Cut-off Price"

Always select the "Cut-off Price" box. If you bid below the final issue price on popular IPOs, your application gets rejected immediately.

💳 PAN Name Match

Your Demat account PAN name must match your Bank/UPI account name. Third-party payments (e.g. paying with a spouse's UPI) get auto-rejected.

⚠️ SME Capital Warning

SME IPOs require ₹1.2L+ minimum capital and must be traded in full lots (no single shares). Only apply if you understand the liquidity risks.
